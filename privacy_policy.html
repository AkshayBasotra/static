Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 23, 2025

Welcome to InvoPilot. We are based in India and respect your privacy. This privacy policy outlines how we handle your information when you use our app and explains your rights. Your data privacy is important to us.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect or store any personal data on our servers. All user data — including business, client, and invoice information — is stored locally on your device. If you choose to subscribe to our premium service, your data may be backed up securely to your personal iCloud account to enable access across Apple devices.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our service
  • To notify you about changes to our app
  • To provide customer support
  • To gather analysis or valuable information so that we can improve our app
  • To monitor the usage of our app
  • To detect, prevent and address technical issues

3. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored locally on your device. For premium users, data may be backed up to iCloud to ensure availability if the device is changed or restored. We do not store any personal data on our servers. Our app uses encryption and best practices to secure your data locally and during any iCloud transfer.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • With your consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect and defend our rights and property
  • To prevent or investigate possible wrongdoing in connection with the app

5. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to correct inaccurate data
  • Right to delete your data
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to withdraw consent

6. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

8. Contact Us

9. California Privacy Rights

Our app does not collect, store, or share personal data on our servers. All information remains on your device or, if you opt for premium features, is backed up to your personal iCloud account. As such, we do not sell personal information or share it with third parties, and the rights granted under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA) are not applicable.

10. International Users

If you are using our app from outside India, please note that all your data is stored on your device. We do not transmit or store any personal data on external servers. Premium users who use iCloud may be subject to Apple’s data storage policies.

11. Data Retention

We will retain your personal data only for as long as is necessary for the purposes set out in this privacy policy. We will retain and use your data to the extent necessary to comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our policies.

Our app may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. We encourage you to read the privacy policy of every website you visit.
